About Dock & Root Labs

A free, community-driven cybersecurity lab platform built around Docker.

Mission

Hands-On Learning.
Zero Cost.

Dock & Root Labs was created to give everyone access to real, practical cybersecurity training without needing expensive VMs, cloud subscriptions, or proprietary platforms.

Every lab is open source, runs locally on your machine using Docker, and focuses on skills that matter in the real world.

What We Stand For

🔓

Open Source

Every lab and this website are open source on GitHub. Fork, contribute, improve.

🎓

Education First

Labs are designed to teach, not just test. Writeups and explanations are always encouraged.

🐳

Docker Native

Container-first design. No heavy VMs. Quick setup, clean teardown.

🌍

Community-Driven

Labs are created by the community. Anyone can contribute a challenge.

🤝

Want to Contribute?

We welcome lab contributions from the community. If you have an idea for a Docker-based challenge, open an issue or submit a pull request to our GitHub organization.
