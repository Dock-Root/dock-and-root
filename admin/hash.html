Flag Hasher

Generate the SHA-256 hash of a flag before storing it in the CMS. Paste the hash — never the raw flag — into the "Flag Hash" field.

Next step: Go to Admin → Machines, open the machine entry, and paste this hash into the "Flag Hash (SHA-256)" field.

🔒 Security: This tool runs entirely in your browser — the flag never leaves your device. Do not store raw flags in the repo or CMS. Only the hash is safe to commit.
